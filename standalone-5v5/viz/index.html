standalone 5-a-side · reinforcement learning · zero deps

Watch a policy learn to pass, carry, and finish.

A hermetic 5-a-side sim — goalkeepers, a ~7m net, no formation LP/IPM. It's still a POMDP — each outfielder acts on a lean ~34-dim partial, egocentric observation (not the engine's ~129-dim feature stack) with a reactive, memoryless policy. The four outfielders learn only the macro families pass · dribble · shoot · clear · hold and chase · support · spread · mark; off-ball players run into open space and the ball-carrier passes to the teammate in space, then shooting uses a short-horizon aim optimizer that places the ball at the corner away from the keeper. A shared-weight policy (PPO + GAE, hand-rolled MLP) trains against a scripted baseline. It goes from flailing to a coherent press, move-it-through-space, and finish game.
